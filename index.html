Simplify Life with Our Innovative Smart Wallets

Arista Vault is dedicated to creating smart wallets that offer advanced features to enhance your everyday life. With our innovative designs and cutting-edge technology, we aim to simplify your life and provide you with peace of mind.

Ring My Phone/Wallet

Effortlessly locate both your wallet and phone. Simply activate the "Ring My Phone" function on your phone to make your wallet ring, or vice versa.

Location Tracking

Track down your misplaced wallet with ease. Utilize the app to pinpoint its last known location, ensuring you can always find your essentials.

Anti Lost / Anti Theft Alarm (20 m)

Never lose track of your valuables again. Aritavault's built-in alarm sounds if your wallet strays beyond a 20-meter range, providing instant peace of mind.

3000mAh Power Charging

Stay powered up on the go. This integrated 3000mAh power bank ensures your phone and other devices stay charged throughout your adventures.

RFID Protection

Guard your financial information. Aritavault's RFID blocking technology safeguards your credit cards and contactless payment methods from unauthorized scans.

Remote Selfie Button

Elevate your selfie game! This innovative feature allows you to control your phone's camera remotely using the wallet's button (if applicable).

How it works ?

  • Open App: Launch the Arista Tracker App and tap “ Add tracker” button.
  • Press & Hold: Long press your wallet button near your phone (within 10cm).
  • Scan & Connect: The app will automatically detect your wallet.
  • Name & Type: Choose a name and select the wallet type in the app.
  • Alerts: Enable phone alerts and airport prompts for an enhanced experience.
